Online Patient Self-Booking

Over 60% of dental patients prefer booking online. Chairside provides a secure patient portal matching your availability. No login required for patients, keeping appointment scheduling seamless even after office hours.

Automated Hygiene Recall SMS

Hygienist chairs are the financial engine of your clinic. Stop calling patients from physical lists. Chairside automatically reaches out via HIPAA-compliant SMS, letting overdue patients book in 2 clicks.
